How Automachine Works

Our AI-powered platform makes watch grading effortless and accurate in just three simple steps.

1

Upload Photos

Simply upload 3-6 images of your watch from different angles (front, back, sides). Our system accepts JPG, JPEG, PNG, WEBP, or HEIC formats.

2

AI Analysis

Automachine's AI identifies the watch brand and model, then evaluates its condition using multi-model analysis, all in seconds.

3

Get Your Report

Receive a detailed condition grade (e.g., 4/5 Excellent), a list of any noted flaws, and a professional description ready for resale listings.

Our Story

Precision Engineering for Watch Professionals

Automachine was founded by a solo watch enthusiast who saw the need for a faster, tech-driven way to grade and list watches. As a one-person company, every detail of this platform is crafted with care and passion for horology and cutting-edge AI.

The name "Automachine" reflects our dual inspiration: the automation of modern AI technology and the intricate machinery of automatic watches. Just as a fine timepiece combines precision engineering with elegant design, our platform brings Swiss-level accuracy to the digital realm.

Our Mission

The Technology Behind Automachine

Our proprietary AI system was trained on thousands of professional watch condition assessments across all major brands and models. The technology combines computer vision for flaw detection with natural language processing to generate comprehensive reports.

Unlike generic image recognition systems, Automachine was specifically engineered for horology, capable of identifying subtle condition issues that affect watch value - from case polishing to dial patina to bracelet stretch.
