How Automachine Works

Our AI-powered platform makes watch grading effortless and accurate in just three simple steps.

1

Upload Photos

Simply upload 3-6 images of your watch from different angles (front, back, sides). Our system accepts JPG, JPEG, PNG, WEBP, or HEIC formats.

2

AI Analysis

Automachine's AI identifies the watch brand and model, then evaluates its condition using multi-model analysis, all in seconds.

3

Get Your Report

Receive a detailed condition grade (e.g., 4/5 Excellent), a list of any noted flaws, and a professional description ready for resale listings.
